# Privacy Policy

Funder Discovery helps nonprofit fundraisers identify and prioritize foundation prospects.

Users may provide information about their organization, mission, programs, geography, funding needs, budget context, desired grant size, current funders, and relationship context. The service uses that information to search public and third-party philanthropy data sources, including public IRS 990-derived data, foundation profile data, and public grant opportunity data.

The service may transmit organization profile details and search terms to upstream data services to perform funder discovery, fit scoring, brief generation, and pipeline generation.

Do not submit confidential donor strategy, sensitive personal information, private board deliberations, protected client information, or information you are not authorized to share.

The service does not guarantee grant eligibility, invitation status, application deadlines, funder interest, or funding. Users should verify current foundation guidelines, contact information, deadlines, eligibility requirements, and grant restrictions before outreach.
