# Sample Privacy Policy Language

This is starter language, not legal advice.

Funder Discovery helps nonprofit fundraisers identify and prioritize foundation prospects. Users provide information about their organization, mission, programs, geography, funding needs, and relationship context. The service uses that information to search public and third-party philanthropy data sources, including public IRS 990-derived data, foundation profile data, and public grant opportunity data.

Do not submit confidential donor strategy, sensitive personal information, private board deliberations, or information you are not authorized to share.

The service may transmit the organization profile and search terms to upstream data services to perform funder discovery, fit scoring, and pipeline generation. The service does not guarantee funding, eligibility, invitation status, or current application deadlines. Users should verify current foundation guidelines, contact information, and grant requirements before outreach.
